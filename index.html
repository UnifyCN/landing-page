FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unify?
Unify is an all-in-one settlement companion for newcomers to Canada. It combines a personalised Canadian Checklist, an AI Companion for everyday questions, a local Community feed, and Learn lessons on healthcare, documentation, education, and employment — all in one app.
Who is Unify best for?
International students, new permanent residents, temporary workers, and refugee claimants settling in Canada. If you're in your first year and still figuring out how healthcare, banking, transit, or school systems work — Unify is built for you.
How do I connect with other newcomers on Unify?
The Community tab matches you with people nearby who share your home country, language, or interests. You can join city-specific circles, ask questions, and RSVP to meetups hosted by partner settlement organisations.
What workshops does Unify offer, and how can I join one?
We run weekly workshops with partners like MOSAIC, ISSofBC, and YMCA Newcomers — covering resume writing, navigating BC's healthcare system, tenancy rights, and more. Open the Community tab and tap "Workshops" to see what's coming up in your city and RSVP with one tap.
Is Unify free to use?
Yes. The Checklist, Community, and Learn modules are free forever. A premium tier unlocks unlimited Companion conversations and document review — but you can get settled without paying a cent.
